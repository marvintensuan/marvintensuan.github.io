# About me



Growing up in a poor household, I was so happy when my dad got me my first laptop.

Having no internet whatsoever, I would usually go to a nearby internet café to search the web, save some web pages as html files in my diskette for me to read at home. I was 12 when I first searched on Yahoo! the words "how to make my own video game". It took me to Alex Allain's cprogramming.com. But it wasn't until I was on my second year in high school that I had a proper introduction to programming through Pascal.

Just to make things clear, I am years old!

# Now is better than later. Later is better than never.



Eventually, I joined our school's robotics club and compete in contests such as Tagisang Robotics (2011) and Philippine Robotics Olympiad (2012).

My love affair for programming would be put on hold to focus on my degree in accounting. My laptop also died in 2018 and wouldn't be replaced until two years later.

I would rekindle with my enthusiasm for programming when I picked up VBA and Python for work.

HackerRank Badges that hopefully count.
